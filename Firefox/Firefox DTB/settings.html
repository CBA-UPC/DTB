DTB+ settings

Blocking behaviour

DTB+ uses two methods to block trackers: AI and hash-blocking algorithms.

AI blocking mode
Weak Block content when there is at least 90% of chance the content contains tracking.
Normal Block content when there is at least 75% of chance the content contains tracking.
Hard (Recommended) Block content when there is at least 60% of chance the content contains tracking.
Extreme Block content when there is at least 50% of chance the content contains tracking.
Scan only for scripts

Media content, such as images, can contain tracking techniques.
